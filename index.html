Play by Play

At 5pm on August 9th 1993, "Billy" careened his premium golf cart through the doors of the Gibonston, Florida Walmart. The Store was packed with innocent customers who had to flee for their lives. Whether it was a miracle for the customers, or pure lack of driving skill from "Billy", not a single customer was incured during the hour plus rampage.

Crash and Burn

After allowing "Billy" to rampage for over an hour, the Police finally arrived to bring the scene under control. Their arrival startled "Billy" who proceeded to crash the premium golf cart into the nearest cash register. The police were then able to stroll over to "Billy" and taze him before taking him into custody.

To this day "Billy Bobby Billybob" claims to have been under then control of the alien overlords who had abducted him the previous week.

There is another story from the same day of a person being attacked over a golf cart parking space... Could this be related?!?!
